Buckwheat Salat

• ¾ cup buckwheat, uncooked
• 1 and ½ cups water
• 1 and ½ cups grape tomatoes, halved
• 1 and ½ cups mini cucumbers, sliced
• ½ cup red onion, diced
• ½ cup feta cheese, cubed
• 2-3 tablespoons fresh parsley or mint, chopped
• 3 tablespoons olive oil
• ¼ teaspoon sea salt
• ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Rinse the buckwheat and place in a pot Add water and a pinch of salt and bring to a boil over high heat. Turn
the heat down to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until cooked. If the water has not all been absorbed
drain the buckwheat in a colander. Set aside and let it cool completely.

Transfer the cooled buckwheat to a large serving bowl. Add all the remaining ingredients, and toss to combine.
Taste and season with more salt and pepper, if needed.
