Cucumber Salad

Ingredients • 1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
• ½ large English cucumber, sliced and quartered
• 1 avocado, chopped
• ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
• 1 + ½ tablespoons lemon juice
• 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
• ¼ teaspoon salt
• ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

How To Do The great thing about this salad is that you can make a big batch at once and store it
in airtight container or divide and store in meal prep containers for lunches
throughout the week. I would recommend keeping the dressing separate and only add it to the salad
that you are consuming that day. This will keep the veggies super crisp.
